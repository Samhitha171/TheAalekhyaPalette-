My Story

Ever since I picked up my first brush and color pencil, art has been more than a passion — it's been a form of escape from reality, a way of expressing feelings, stories, and the beauty of everyday life.

"Portraits hold a special place in my heart. There's something deeply moving about capturing the soul of a person through a gaze, a shadow, a subtle tilt of the head."

While I explore many different art forms and subjects, portraits are where I feel most connected to my work. I strive to capture the unspoken — a quiet smile, a thoughtful gaze, the cultural resonance of a traditional outfit. I hope to invite viewers into moments of calm reflection and heartfelt emotion.

Outside of painting, you'll often find me lost in a book, sipping hot chocolate in café corners, or wandering through the aisles of a tucked-away bookstore. There's something magical about discovering new stories — whether through pages or paint.

Welcome to The Aalekhya Palette. I'm so glad you're here. ✨
